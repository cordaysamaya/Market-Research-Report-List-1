<p><p>The epoxy primer market is divided into two main types: pure epoxy paint and modified epoxy paint. Pure epoxy paint offers exceptional adhesion, corrosion resistance, and durability, making it ideal for industrial applications and high-performance coatings. In contrast, modified epoxy paint incorporates additives that enhance characteristics like flexibility, curing speed, or UV resistance, making it suitable for a wider range of applications, including residential and decorative uses. Both types cater to specific performance needs across various industries and environments.</p></p>
