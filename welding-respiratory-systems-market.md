<p><p>The welding respiratory systems market comprises several types designed to protect welders from hazardous fumes and particles. Powered Air-Purifying Respirators (PAPR) use a battery-powered fan to provide clean air, while Supplied Air Respirators deliver a continuous supply of breathable air from a remote source. Disposable Welding Respirators are single-use masks designed for short-term protection, often cost-effective for less frequent tasks. Additionally, the "Others" category includes alternative respirators like half-masks and full-face masks tailored for varying welding environments.</p></p>
