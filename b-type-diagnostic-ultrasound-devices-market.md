<p><strong>The B-type Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fanshaped Scanning</li><li>Linear Scanning</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>B-type diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized by their scanning methods. Fanshaped scanning utilizes a wide-angle beam to capture images over a broad area, making it ideal for visualizing large organs. Linear scanning employs a straight-line array to produce high-resolution images of superficial structures, offering detailed views of tissues and vessels. Mixed type devices combine both fan-shaped and linear scanning capabilities, providing versatility in imaging various tissues and conditions, thus enhancing diagnostic accuracy and application in clinical settings.</p></p>
