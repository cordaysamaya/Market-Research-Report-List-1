<p><ul><li>Binocular Loupes</li><li>Monocular Loupes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic loupes market is segmented into binocular and monocular loupes. Binocular loupes feature two optical pathways, providing depth perception and a wide field of view, making them ideal for precision tasks like surgeries and detailed examinations. Monocular loupes, on the other hand, have a single optical pathway, offering a more straightforward, lightweight option for general eye care and routine examinations. Both types are essential tools for enhancing visibility and accuracy in various ophthalmic procedures.</p></p>
