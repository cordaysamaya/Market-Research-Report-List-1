<p><p>Occlusion microcatheters are specialized medical devices used in interventional procedures, particularly in the field of neurology and vascular treatments. Their design allows for precise navigation through the complex network of blood vessels, enhancing the efficacy of minimally invasive procedures.</p><p>The Occlusion Microcatheter Market is poised for significant growth, driven by rising incidences of neurovascular diseases, an increase in the aging population, and advancements in medical technology.
