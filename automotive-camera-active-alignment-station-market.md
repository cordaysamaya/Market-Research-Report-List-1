<p><p>The Automotive Camera Active Alignment Station market is categorized by degrees of freedom (DOF) in alignment capabilities. </p><p>- **4 DOF** generally refers to alignment that accounts for three-dimensional positioning and rotation, suitable for basic calibration tasks. </p><p>- **5 DOF** adds an additional rotational axis, enhancing precision for complex camera configurations. </p><p>- **6 DOF** offers the highest level of adjustment, allowing for comprehensive calibration across all axes, essential for advanced driver-assistance systems and autonomous vehicles. Each type provides varying levels of accuracy and flexibility for camera integration in automotive applications.</p></p>
