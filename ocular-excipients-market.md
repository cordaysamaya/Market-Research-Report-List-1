<p><p>The ocular excipients market encompasses various types of additives that enhance the formulation of ophthalmic products. Dilutents increase bulk volume, while glidants improve flow properties, ensuring uniformity and consistency. Disintegrants facilitate the breakdown of tablets, whereas binders provide cohesion to tablet components. Lubricants reduce friction during production, enhancing processing efficiency. Tablet coatings and films protect formulations and control drug release, while coloring agents enhance aesthetic appeal and identification. Together, these excipients optimize performance and stability in ocular formulations.</p></p>
