<p><p>Cloud management solutions are categorized into four main market types: </p><p>1. **Public Cloud** refers to services offered over the internet, accessible to multiple users and organizations, typically managed by third-party providers. </p><p>2. **Private Cloud** involves dedicated resources for a single organization, allowing for enhanced security and control.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Cloud** combines public and private clouds, offering flexibility and scalability while maintaining sensitive data on private infrastructure.</p><p>4. **Multi-Cloud** utilizes multiple cloud services from different providers to avoid vendor lock-in and optimize performance.</p></p>
