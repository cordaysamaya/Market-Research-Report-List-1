<p><ul><li>Lamination Type</li><li>Vaccum Metallization Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallized papers market is primarily categorized into two types: lamination type and vacuum metallization type. Lamination type involves bonding a thin layer of metal onto the paper surface, enhancing durability and providing a glossy finish. In contrast, vacuum metallization type entails depositing metal vapor in a vacuum environment, creating a uniform and reflective coating. Both methods improve barrier properties and aesthetics, making metallized papers popular in packaging, labeling, and decorative applications across various industries.</p></p>
