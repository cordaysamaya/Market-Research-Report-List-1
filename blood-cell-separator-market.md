<p><p>The blood cell separator market is divided into two main types: single component for blood transfusion and therapeutic blood components. Single component separators are designed to isolate specific blood components, such as red blood cells or platelets, for transfusion purposes, ensuring patients receive the exact type needed for treatment. Therapeutic blood component separators focus on extracting components for medical therapies, such as platelet or plasma exchange, used in managing diseases like thrombocytopenia or certain plasma disorders.</p></p>
