<p><strong>The VOC Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC gas meter market includes various technologies, primarily Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID meters utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, providing highly sensitive and accurate readings for volatile organic compounds. In contrast, MOS sensors operate by changing resistance in the presence of VOCs, offering a more cost-effective solution but with lower sensitivity and selectivity. Both types serve crucial roles in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and indoor air quality assessment.</p></p>
