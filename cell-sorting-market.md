<p><p>The cell sorting market encompasses several advanced technologies. Magnetic-Activated Cell Sorting (MACS) employs magnetic nanoparticles to separate cells with targeted surface markers. MEMS-Microfluidics integrates microelectromechanical systems for precise manipulation of small fluid volumes, enhancing cell sorting efficiency. Together, these technologies enable researchers to obtain pure cell populations for various applications in diagnostics, therapeutics, and biological research.</p></p>
