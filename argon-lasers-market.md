<p><p>The Argon laser market comprises primarily two types: Argon Ion Lasers and Argon Excimer Lasers. Argon Ion Lasers are used in various applications, including medical procedures and industrial cutting, known for their continuous wave output and vibrant blue-green light. In contrast, Argon Excimer Lasers utilize gas mixtures to produce short, high-energy pulses, making them ideal for precision applications like ophthalmic surgeries and semiconductor fabrication. Both types contribute significantly to sectors such as healthcare, manufacturing, and scientific research.</p></p>
