<p><p>The basalt fiber market comprises three primary types: chopped fiber, twisted roving, and twistless roving. Chopped fiber is utilized for reinforcement in composites and construction materials, offering strength and durability. Twisted roving consists of multiple strands twisted together, providing excellent flexibility and tensile strength, suitable for various industrial applications. Twistless roving, characterized by its untwisted format, offers superior compatibility for resin infusion processes, making it ideal for advanced composite structures in aerospace and automotive sectors.</p></p>
