<p><p>The Bentonite market is primarily categorized into Sodium Bentonite and Calcium Bentonite. Sodium Bentonite, known for its high swelling properties, is commonly used in drilling fluids, foundry sands, and wastewater treatment. In contrast, Calcium Bentonite, with its lower swelling capacity, is often utilized in applications like cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and as a natural binder in various industries. Both types serve distinct purposes, with Sodium Bentonite dominating industrial applications while Calcium Bentonite is favored in consumer-focused markets.</p></p>
