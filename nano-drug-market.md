<p><strong>The Nano-Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liposomes</li><li>Polymeric Micelles</li><li>Solid lipid Nanoparticles</li><li>Microemulsion and Nanoemulsion</li><li>Nanosuspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano-drug market encompasses various delivery systems aimed at enhancing drug solubility, stability, and targeting. Liposomes are lipid-based vesicles that encapsulate drugs for improved absorption. Polymeric micelles utilize amphiphilic polymers to solubilize hydrophobic drugs. Solid lipid nanoparticles combine lipid matrices with drugs for controlled release. Microemulsions are thermodynamically stable mixtures enhancing bioavailability, while nanoemulsions are fine oil-in-water dispersions for delivering hydrophobic medications. Nanosuspensions improve the solubility of poorly water-soluble drugs, enhancing therapeutic efficacy.</p></p>
