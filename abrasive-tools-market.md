<p><ul><li>Natural</li><li>Synthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The abrasive tools market is divided into two main types: natural and synthetic abrasives. Natural abrasives are derived from minerals and include materials like garnet and emery, known for their strength and durability. Synthetic abrasives, on the other hand, are man-made and include products like aluminum oxide and silicon carbide, which offer consistent performance and customization in various applications. Both types are essential in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and automotive, where surface finishing and material removal are critical processes.</p></p>
