<p><ul><li>Knife Edge Follower</li><li>Roller Follower</li><li>Flat Follower</li><li>Spherical Follower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cam followers market includes various types, each serving distinct applications. Knife edge followers have a sharp edge for precise movement, ideal for low-load situations. Roller followers utilize a cylindrical roller for reduced friction and wear, suitable for high-speed operations. Flat followers feature a flat surface for broader contact, enhancing stability and load distribution. Spherical followers offer a rounded contact surface, allowing for angular misalignment. These types cater to different mechanical needs in industries like automotive and machinery, ensuring efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
