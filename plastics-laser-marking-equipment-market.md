<p><p>The plastics laser marking equipment market includes two primary types: CO2 type and solid-state type. CO2 lasers utilize a gas mixture to produce a high-quality beam, ideal for marking non-metallic materials like plastics, offering deep engraving and precise graphics. Solid-state lasers, on the other hand, use a solid crystal as a medium and are known for their efficiency and durability, providing excellent performance in high-speed applications. Both types cater to diverse marking needs across various industries, enhancing product customization and traceability.</p></p>
