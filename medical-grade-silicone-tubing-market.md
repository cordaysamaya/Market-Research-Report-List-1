<p><strong>The Medical Grade Silicone Tubing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platinum Vulcanization</li><li>Peroxide Vulcanization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical-grade silicone tubing market is primarily divided into two types: platinum vulcanization and peroxide vulcanization. </p><p>Platinum vulcanization, also known as addition curing, offers superior biocompatibility and is ideal for applications requiring sterilization. It provides high flexibility and lower environmental impact. </p><p>Peroxide vulcanization, or free radical curing, is typically more cost-effective and provides better mechanical strength, making it suitable for applications requiring durability. Both types cater to diverse medical applications, including drug delivery and surgical procedures.</p></p>
