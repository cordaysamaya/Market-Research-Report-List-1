<p><p>The ice melter market is divided into liquid and solid types, catering to different use cases. Liquid ice melters, often faster-acting, can be sprayed directly on icy surfaces for immediate effects, making them ideal for rapid de-icing in commercial applications. Solid ice melters, typically in granular form, dissolve slowly, providing prolonged melting action, which is effective for residential use and large areas. Both types are essential for enhancing safety and accessibility during winter conditions.</p></p>
