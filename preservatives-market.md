<p><p>The preservatives market includes various types tailored to specific applications. Organic solvent preservatives, often derived from synthetic sources, are used to prevent microbial growth in products like paints and coatings. Inorganic preservatives, typically mineral-based, provide stability and longevity in food and cosmetic products. Natural antiseptics, sourced from plant extracts, offer a safer alternative for preserving products and have gained popularity in personal care. Ester-type preservatives, formed from organic acids and alcohols, are widely used in cosmetics and food for their effective antimicrobial properties.</p></p>
