<p><ul><li>Authentication Packaging Technology</li><li>Track and Trace Packaging Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-counterfeit packaging technologies market is primarily divided into two types: authentication packaging technology and track and trace packaging technology. Authentication packaging employs features like holograms, unique codes, or QR codes to confirm the product's legitimacy. On the other hand, track and trace packaging technology monitors the product's journey through the supply chain using RFID, barcodes, or GPS tracking, ensuring its origin and movement can be traced. Both technologies aim to enhance product security and reduce the impact of counterfeit goods.</p></p>
