<p><p>The robotic palletizing system market is divided into two main types: floor/low-level palletizers and high-level palletizers. Floor/low-level palletizers are designed to operate at ground level, making them ideal for cases with limited vertical space, offering ease of integration and lower setup costs. In contrast, high-level palletizers are engineered to stack products at elevated heights, optimizing storage and maximizing warehouse space. Both types enhance efficiency in material handling and reduce labor costs across various industries.</p></p>
