<p><p>Microsurgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are critical tools in minimally invasive procedures, utilizing high-frequency electric currents for tissue incision, coagulation, and hemostasis. The market comprises three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single electrode to pass current through the tissue; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, featuring two electrodes for localized tissue effects; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels and reducing bleeding. These technologies enhance precision, safety, and efficiency in surgical practices.</p></p>
