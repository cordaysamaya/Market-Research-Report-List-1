<p><strong>The Semiconductor Testing Boards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ProbeCard</li><li>LoadBoard</li><li>Burn-inBoard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor testing boards market consists of three main types: probe cards, load boards, and burn-in boards. Probe cards are used to electrically test wafers during manufacturing, ensuring each chip functions correctly. Load boards facilitate the testing of assembled chips in various conditions, helping verify their performance in real-world applications. Burn-in boards subject devices to elevated temperatures and stresses over time, identifying early failures and ensuring reliability. Together, these boards enhance quality control throughout the semiconductor production process.</p></p>
