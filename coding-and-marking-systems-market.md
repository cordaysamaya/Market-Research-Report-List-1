<p><strong>The Coding and Marking Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet (CIJ)</li><li>Piezo Inkjet (PIJ)</li><li>Thermal Inkjet (TIJ)</li><li>Laser Coding</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coding and marking systems market includes several technologies for product identification and traceability. Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) printers offer high-speed marking on various surfaces. Piezo Inkjet (PIJ) systems utilize piezoelectric elements for precise drops of ink, suitable for variable data. Thermal Inkjet (TIJ) employs heat to create ink droplets, ideal for short runs and high-resolution prints. Laser coding uses focused light for permanent markings, providing durability. Other methods include label printers and dot matrix systems, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
