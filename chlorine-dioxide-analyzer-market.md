<p><p>The Chlorine Dioxide Analyzer market encompasses two primary types: Amperometry and Photometry. Amperometric analyzers work by measuring the electrical current produced when chlorine dioxide is oxidized at a sensor electrode, providing real-time monitoring with high sensitivity. In contrast, Photometric analyzers utilize light absorption principles, detecting the concentration of chlorine dioxide by measuring the intensity of light transmitted through a sample. Both methods serve critical roles in water treatment and industrial processes, ensuring safety and compliance with environmental standards.</p></p>
