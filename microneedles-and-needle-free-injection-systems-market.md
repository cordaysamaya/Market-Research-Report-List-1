<p><p>Microneedles and needle-free injection systems are innovative technologies aimed at delivering drugs and vaccines with reduced pain and improved patient compliance. Microneedles are tiny, minimally invasive needles that penetrate the skin's outer layer, allowing the administration of medication with lower discomfort compared to traditional hypodermic needles. Needle-free injection systems use high-pressure to deliver a liquid substance through the skin without a conventional needle, making them particularly appealing for those averse to needles.</p><p>The market for these technologies is witnessing significant growth due to increasing demand for painless and efficient drug delivery methods, rising prevalence of chronic diseases, and advancements in pharmaceutical formulations.
