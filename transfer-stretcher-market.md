<p><p>The transfer stretcher market is categorized into three main types: electric stretchers, hydraulic stretchers, and manual transfer stretchers. Electric stretchers provide powered lifting and adjusting capabilities, enhancing ease of use for medical personnel. Hydraulic stretchers utilize a hydraulic system for height adjustment, offering stability and efficient transfers. Manual transfer stretchers rely on human effort for adjustments and mobility, making them more cost-effective but labor-intensive. Each type caters to different operational needs in healthcare settings, emphasizing convenience and patient safety.</p></p>
