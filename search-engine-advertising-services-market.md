<p><p>Search Engine Advertising Services encompass various pricing models tailored to advertiser goals. CPC (Cost-Per-Click) charges advertisers each time a user clicks on their ads, ideal for driving traffic. CPM (Cost-Per-Thousand-Impressions) focuses on visibility, charging per thousand ad impressions. CPA (Cost-Per-Acquisition) charges only when a specified action is completed, optimizing for conversions. CPT (Cost-Per-Time) costs are based on advertising duration. Other models may include performance-based options, offering flexibility based on varying marketing objectives and strategies.</p></p>
