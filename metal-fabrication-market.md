<p><p>The metal fabrication market encompasses various processes vital for shaping and joining metal components. Metal welding involves fusing materials through heat, while metal machining refers to removing material for precise shapes. Metal forming reshapes metal typically in bulk, and metal shearing cuts sheets to desired sizes. Metal cutting is used for more intricate shapes, whereas metal folding infers bending sheets into specific angles. Metal rolling shapes metal into sheets or profiles, while metal stamping creates parts from flat sheets, and metal punching forms holes in materials.</p></p>
