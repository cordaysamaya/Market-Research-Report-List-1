<p><ul><li>Vortex Flowmeters</li><li>Differential-pressure Flowmeters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam meter market encompasses various types including vortex flowmeters, differential-pressure flowmeters, and others. Vortex flowmeters measure fluid flow based on vortex shedding, offering high accuracy in steam applications. Differential-pressure flowmeters determine flow rate by measuring pressure differences across a constriction in the pipeline, widely used for steam measurement due to reliability. Other types may include thermal mass flowmeters and ultrasonic flowmeters, each providing unique advantages in specific conditions. Overall, these technologies are essential for efficient steam management and processing in various industries.</p></p>
