<p><strong>The Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EHPS</li><li>P-EHPS</li><li>R-EHPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering Systems (EHPS) encompass various types, including Column-EHPS (C-EHPS), Pump-EHPS (P-EHPS), and Rack-EHPS (R-EHPS). C-EHPS integrates electric assistance within the steering column, enhancing responsiveness, while P-EHPS combines hydraulic pumps with electric motors, offering efficiency and adaptability. R-EHPS is designed with an electric motor directly into the steering rack, providing precise control and weight savings. These variants cater to diverse automotive applications, improving fuel efficiency and steering performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>
